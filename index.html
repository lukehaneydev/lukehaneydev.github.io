What is Spells 'n' Stuff?



Spells 'n' stuff is an online wizard arena-style VR-only shooter, for the HTC Vive. Become a wizard as you battle against your friends, fire spells at each other and teleport using throwable potions!



How does the game work?



A host creates a lobby and then selects a map and the round begins. At the start of the round, armed with a wand and teleporting potions, everyone is invisible to each other and will have to teleport to a starting position, unknowing where their friends will end up, which could be right by them.

You aim with your wand at your friends head and fire a spell, you get 2 points for each hit, if someone is hit 3 times, they die and become invisible (a ghost) for 10 seconds, allowing them time to get away from their attacker.

A round ends when someone reaches 100 points, and they return to the lobby
