Spells 'n' Stuff supports NAT Punchthrough, meaning, if your router supports it, you can host without port forwarding!

Wizards

Take on the role as a wizard as you battle it out against your friends on various maps in strange and wonderful locations.

Wands

Cast a spell and try to hit your opponent's head. Hold trigger and it charges up your spell so you can fire an even faster shot!

Potions

Throw your potions to teleport around! Ghost mode activates if you are hit three times, so you better teleport away!
